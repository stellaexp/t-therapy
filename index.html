Therapy Services

My Approach

I offer professional, confidential, integrative counselling for adults across the UK via secure online sessions, and limited in-person sessions in South West London / Surrey.

What I Offer

  • A safe, non-judgemental space to talk
  • Bespoke integrative therapy
  • Exploration of thoughts, feelings and behaviours
  • Art-based techniques, mindfulness and embodiment (where appropriate)

Common Reasons to Seek Therapy

  • Anxiety and panic
  • Depression and low mood
  • Stress and burnout
  • Loss, grief, bereavement
  • Trauma and past experiences
  • Self-esteem and identity issues
  • Life transitions or change
